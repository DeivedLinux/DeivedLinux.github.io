About Me

curriculum

My name is Deived William, I was born in November 1994 in the city of Rio de Janeiro. When I was younger I wanted to know how things worked, and then I decided to take an electronics course in Senai and I confess that I was very much in love with the area. I have skills in hardware and software development. In the hardware part I usually use altium designer software in the development of digital (discrete, microcontrolled and fpga's) and analog circuits, my most complex project was a 32 bit console using an STM microcontroller. In software development I have skills in C / C ++, Ruby, Python, Java; x86 and MIPS in the low level part, VHDL as description language. Get my curriculum for more details.
